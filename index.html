My Favorite Song

By Devin



What is it and why is it my favorite song?

My favorite song is Party in the CIA by Weird Al and it is a parody song of Party In the USA. It is my favorite song because I like the tune and I love Police/Military type stuff. It has amazing lyrics and an amazing singer. I also love it because it is accurate in history when it shows off certain CIA methods used in the cold war.
